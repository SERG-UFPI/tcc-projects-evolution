http://expats.qa is a resource for expats of all experience levels to exchange knowledge.

Having been active on a few other expat-related sites (most notably Sovereign Man Confidential and International Man), I got frustrated by some of the limitations I found with traditional forum-based knowledge sharing.

Askbot is a fantastic alternative to the traditional threaded discussion format:

  • No open-ended discussions; the focus is exclusively on questions and answers.
  • A karma system makes it possible to have an effectively self-moderated community.
  • Tags are first-class citizens, making it easier for individuals with similar interests to find each other.

The site is in beta for the next few months until we've reached a critical mass of new users and experts. We look forward to seeing you there!
